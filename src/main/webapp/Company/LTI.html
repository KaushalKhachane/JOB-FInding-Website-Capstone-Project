Larsen & Toubro Infotech
Information Technology company

Larsen & Toubro Infotech Limited is an Indian multinational information technology services and consulting company based in Mumbai, India. In 2017, NASSCOM ranked LTI as the sixth-largest Indian IT services company in terms of export revenues.

* Mission :

To develop and deliver high quality education and research in project management in consonance with the vision of L&T by providing state-of-the-art infrastructure and learner friendly atmosphere with innovative pedagogy thereby creating a pool of world class and socially responsible project professionals.

* Our Vision :

To develop a premier world class education centre, for creating global project management professionals, thereby making Larsen & Toubro (L&T) a centre of excellence in project management.
