Mindtree
Information technology company

Mindtree Ltd is an Indian multinational information technology services and consulting company, headquartered in Bangalore. It is a part of the Larsen & Toubro Group. Founded in 1999, the company employs over 23,000 employees and has an annual revenue of ₹7839.9 crore.

* Mission :

The mission statement is given as "We engineer meaningful technology solutions to help businesses and societies flourish."

* Our Vision :

The vision statement is given as "Mindtree takes an agile, collaborative approach to create customized solutions across the digital value chain."

* Our Values :

`Collaborative Spirit.
`Unrelenting Dedication.
`Expert Thinking.
